Algorithm
for every unvisited vertex v in G:    pick v, enqueue it (color it 1)    while queue not empty:        u = dequeue        for each neighbor w of u:            if w has no color:                color w opposite of u, enqueue w            else if color(w) == color(u):                FAIL — not 2-colorable            else:                skip (already correct)if no failure: graph is 2-colorable
